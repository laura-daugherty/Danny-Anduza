Who is
Danny Anduza?

Paleontologist Danny Anduza has been excavating and studying dinosaurs for over a decade. Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, with years of experience in research and fieldwork, paleontology is his greatest passion. From the crowded streets of San Francisco to the remote badlands of Montana, his study of fossils informs the way he sees everything — and he’s excited to share that perspective with the world.
